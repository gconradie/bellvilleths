Academic Colours

Academic colours are awarded at our our annual academic prize-giving evening during the first week of the fourth term.

Certificate

Awarded to grade 8 and 9 learners who either obtained
• the highest mark in a subject or
• an average of at least 80% for the year.

Colours

Awarded to grade 10-12 learners who acheived an average of 75% or higher.

Medal

Awarded to the matriculant who achieved the highest average of 75% or more during the previous academic year.

Scrolls

Awarded to grade 10-12 learners who obtained an average of 70% or more.

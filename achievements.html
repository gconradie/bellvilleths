Systemic Tests Award for English

BTHS has been awarded a certificate at the Metropole East Education District Matric Awards 2018 in the following category: GRADE 9 SYSTEMIC RESULTS - Schools achieving a pass rate of 70% or more in languages from 2017-2018 received this award. Ms Hartnick (English Subject Head represented BTHS). 

Best Performing Automotive Teacher

It is with great pride that we share this outstanding achievement. One of our new Automotive, Technical and EGD educator’s, Mr Kumm, was awarded with a Certificate of Achievement by the Department of Education as Best Performing Teacher in Mechanical Technology Automotive Grade 12 for 2018 in the Fezile Dabi District while he was teaching at his previous school – Kroonstad High. 

Bridge Building Competition

HTS Bellville entered 2 teams for the SAICE 2018 Bridge Building Competition at Canal Walk yesterday. Our hatties did very well since this is our first entry since 2009. We ended up 26th out of 39 entries. Very proud of our teams! Many thanks to Mr Bodenstein for coaching the teams. Great job! More pics to follow. Congratulations to Brackenfell High School with the no 1 & 2 spot with Protea Heights Academy no 3!

Cricket - "More than just a game, it's a way of life!"

HATTIES U/19B Cricket team BEATS Elsies High 

Result : 98 run victory for BHTS

Yesterday, 5 February 2019, BHTS won the toss agaist Elsies River High and decided to bat first. Ashwill Horn was on fire scoring 56 in no time before retiring.The captain (Jaybon Johnson) and Ashwinn Katts kept the momentum going with Jaybon scoring 43 not out and Ashwinn 24 (out on the last ball of the innings). BHTS ended on 179-1 after 20.

In reply, the visitors only managed to score 81 with the pick of our bowlers being Otto Landsberg taking 3 for 2 runs in 1.4 overs and Ashwinn Katts taking 3 for 13 runs in 3 overs. Well done boys!

U19B Cricket Team

Bishop Lavis - 30 January 2019 

Our HATTIE U/19B cricket team WON by 4 runs against Bishop Lavis yesterday afternoon on home ground.

BHTS won the toss and chose to bat first and had a very difficult start with their batting innings being 3 down for only 7 runs. The fight back started with the captain, Jaybon Johnson and Channing Daniels who occupied the crease for the next 10 overs.

At the end of the innings BHTS ended on 115 all out with our captain, Jaybon Johnson, top scoring with 61. Our visitors also lost early wickets but fought back losing the game by 4 runs. The pick of the bowlers was Ronin Baadjies taking 4 for 26 in his 4 overs.

Well done to our boys – it is more than just a game, it’s a way of life!

New Indoor Cricket Facility

The SS4LS programme since 2015 has partnered with the school to upgrade the outdoor sporting facilities and the Jock De Jager Sports Center.

This relationship holds only great things in store, as SS4LS has continued in their investment of HTS Belville. This investment builds upon the infrastructure by providing support in areas such as coaching, education and well-being. With the support of headmaster, Michael Koopman and his strong relationship this relationship hold great things in-store.

Mr Koopman at the Opening of our Indoor Cricket Facility

Bellville THS experienced another historic day, as we celebrated the Grand opening of the Jock De Jager indoor cricket facility, and we were privileged to have Mr. De Jager (ex HTSB Principal) present. We look forward to the possibilities that this centre holds for young sports people. 4 September 2017

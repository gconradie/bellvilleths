Head Learners



Luke Aranes, Tasha Petersen, Evile Shuba, Bilqees Khan and TJ Hendricks

Meet Tasha Petersen, Bellville Technical High School’s 2019 head girl

1

What is your philosophy as a leader? 

To be better than the person we were yesterday. 

2

What change do you want to implement in the school? 

Unity, I would like that the teachers and students function together as one unit. 

3

What do want to achieve as a leader and matric learner in 2019? 


As a matric learner, I’d like to achieve outstanding marks.

And as a leader to inspire people to step out of their comfort zone and show others they have potential and integrity. 

4

What are your plans for after matric (2020)? 


Go into a medical field such as a dermatology, maybe even study in another field such as engineering or even law.

Meet Evile Shuba, Bellville Technical High School’s 2019 head boy

1

What are your strengths as a leader?

Working in a team, listening and always willing to learn.

2

What change do you want to implement in the school? 

More school pride and make it a happy place to be at and one’s kids will love to come to every day.

3

What do want to achieve as a leader and matric learner in 2019? 


Making a difference in peoples lives and changing my school for the better.

4

What are your plans for after matric (2020)? 

Studying and playing cricket.
